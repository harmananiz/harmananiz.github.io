Social Media Graphics & Promotional Content

My body of work focuses on designing captivating social media graphics and promotional material that inform and engage ICCIT students. These designs aim to promote workshops, professional speaker series, social events, networking opportunities, conferences, and competitions. By creating visually compelling content, the graphics aim to generate excitement, encourage active student participation, and foster a vibrant and connected ICCIT community.

Vision Journal


As the designer of the magazine cover, my primary objective was to create a visually captivating and engaging cover that would attract the attention of potential readers and effectively represent the content and tone of the publication. My approach involved a series of strategic steps to ensure a successful design outcome. I collaborated closely with the editorial team to gather information on the issue's theme and content. With this knowledge, I generated multiple design concepts, sketched rough layouts, and iterated based on feedback. Using Adobe Photoshop, I executed the chosen concept, paying attention to details such as typography, imagery, and layout. I actively sought feedback from the editorial team, incorporating their input to refine the design. After finalizing the design, I delivered the print-ready files, ensuring they met the necessary specifications.
