Reflet


Project Overview

Introducing Reflet, a sustainable skincare brand that understands the evolving needs of individuals, harnessing the power of online influence to promote self-care and well-being. Inspired by the rise of content creators and the desire for a mindful lifestyle, Reflet stands out through its commitment to sustainability and clean beauty, offering affordable products that not only destress but also support the mental health of communities, particularly those disproportionately affected. Join us on a journey of self-care, where skincare becomes a symbol of resilience and a step towards a more sustainable future.



Branding

Images and visuals are vital for effective branding, as they bring the brand identity to life and make it captivating for social media consumption. The Reflet logo embodies this philosophy with its clever combination of a bold, sans-serif font featuring thick slabs, along with a smaller, bold script/marker font delicately placed at the top edge of the word "Reflet" (meaning reflection in French). This minimalist yet impactful design not only ensures easy readability but also conveys the brand's commitment to skincare innovation through the inclusion of the word "LABO" (short for laboratory). In all our adverts, the logo seamlessly integrates into clean and minimal packaging, perfectly highlighting the functionality and elegance of our products.
