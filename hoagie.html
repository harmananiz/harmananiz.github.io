Hoagie Heroes


Project Overview

Hoagie Heroes is a collaborative board game designed for players aged 10 and above. The game revolves around the concept of running a sandwich shop called Hoagie Express. The goal is to improve the digital presence and funds of the sandwich shop by completing customer orders quickly and accurately, thereby increasing the shop's rating. The game emphasizes teamwork and strategizing, as each player takes on the role of a chef with special skills that can be utilized to achieve success.

Hoagie Heroes is designed to be a family-friendly game that promotes social interaction and enjoyment among players. The target age for the game is 10 and above, as the concepts and scenarios depicted in the game are relatable and easy to comprehend. The game incorporates realistic team dynamics, allowing players to strategize and utilize the unique skills of each chef character to improve their ratings. The game can accommodate 3-5 players, with the ideal number being 5 players. This player count ensures an engaging and dynamic gameplay experience. The estimated game length ranges from 35 minutes to an hour, depending on the players' familiarity with the rules.



Game Mechanics

  • Cooperative play: Players work together towards a common goal.
  • Variable player powers: Each player draws a character card with unique abilities and limitations.
  • Dice rolling: Determines the number of actions a player can take during their turn.
  • Action selection: Players choose actions based on the results of dice rolls.
  • Real-time turns: Turns must be completed within a set time limit.
  • Card drafting: Players gather ingredient cards, with a hand limit of 6 cards.
  • Push their luck: Players can draw more cards to increase their chances of obtaining desired ingredients.
  • Set collection: The correct ingredients need to be collected, emphasizing the importance of gathering specific combinations.
  • Trading: Players can exchange ingredient cards with each other during their turns.
  • Area movement: Players need to navigate the board to be in the right areas to cook ingredients.
  • Pick-up and deliver: Ingredients must be delivered to specific locations on the board.

Rules & How to Win

Hoagie Heroes is a cooperative game where players aim to collect 3 stars by completing customer requests. Failing requests reduces the star meter, and if it drops too low, the game ends. Players have character cards with different skills and starting stations, but can strategically move around later. Turns are determined by a dice, allowing actions like moving, cooking, and drawing cards. Multiple players can work at a station, and timed rounds may be added in future versions for testing. Failing reduces the star meter, while unique skills and station movement add strategic elements.


Conclusion

Once the game mechanics, visuals, and user experience reached a satisfactory level, the final version of Hoagie Heroes was prepared for release. The game underwent thorough quality assurance testing to ensure a bug-free and smooth gaming experience. Marketing materials, including the game's website, promotional assets, and user guides, were developed to support the launch.
